Trade Winds Spice Blend

Trade Winds Spice Blend is a unique blend of herbs and spices, that combine to create the ultimate flavour enhancer, which can be used on a wide variety of foods, on a daily basis, such as steaks, burgers, wild game, chiken, pasta, steamed vegetables, salads, eggs, popcorn, and fantastic on all seafood.

Ingredients:

Salt, Garlic, Sugar, Black Pepper, Onion, Celery Seed, Cumin, Cayenne Pepper, plus other herbs & spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged in 80g and 200g bottles

Salt Free Spice Blend

Absolutely no salt. This spice blend adds wonderful flavour to all foods. Try it on chicken, seafood, eggs, vegetables, soups, pasts and popcorn.

Ingredients:

Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Paprika, Dry Mustard, and other spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged 65g

Trade Winds Pasta Spice

A pre-packaged blend of spices that makes an outstanding gourmet sauce foe spagetti, lasagna, homemade pizza. Add kidney beans for a great chilli and delicious as a dip with nacho's and sour cream. Makes 3 quarts or 120 oz of delicious pasta sauce.

Ingredients:

Salt, Sugar, Basil, Garlic, plus other herbs & spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged in 113g (4oz) kits

Sweet Mustard

Trade Winds Gourmet Mustard is a sweet and hot mustard that can be used on a variety of foods, such as smoked meat, ham, pork, and outstanding on sausages. Check out our recipes for deviled eggs, meat loaves and potato salad. People that hate mustard love our product.

Ingredients:

Prepared Mustard (Distilled Vinegar, Water, No 1 Grade Mustard Seed, Salt, Contains less than 2% of Tumeric, Paprika, Spice, Natural Flavour, Garlic Powder), Vinegar, Bleached Wheat Flour Enriched (Niacin, Reduced Iron, Thalmine, Mononitrate, Riboflavin, Folic Acid), Malted Barley Flour (Contains Wheat, Soy), Sugar, Jalapenos. Packaged in 458g (16oz) bottles
