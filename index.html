Trade Winds Spice Blend

Trade Winds Spice Blend is a unique blend of herbs and spices, that combine to create the ultimate flavour enhancer, which can be used on a wide variety of foods, on a daily basis, such as steaks, burgers, wild game, chiken, pasta, steamed vegetables, salads, eggs, popcorn, and fantastic on all seafood.

Ingredients:

Salt, Garlic, Sugar, Black Pepper, Onion, Celery Seed, Cumin, Cayenne Pepper, plus other herbs & spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged in 80g and 200g bottles

Salt Free Spice Blend

Absolutely no salt. This spice blend adds wonderful flavour to all foods. Try it on chicken, seafood, eggs, vegetables, soups, pasts and popcorn.

Ingredients:

Onion Powder, Garlic Powder, Paprika, Dry Mustard, and other spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged 65g

Trade Winds Pasta Spice

A pre-packaged blend of spices that makes an outstanding gourmet sauce foe spagetti, lasagna, homemade pizza. Add kidney beans for a great chilli and delicious as a dip with nacho's and sour cream. Makes 3 quarts or 120 oz of delicious pasta sauce.

Ingredients:

Salt, Sugar, Basil, Garlic, plus other herbs & spices. Contains no MSG. Packaged in 113g (4oz) kits

Sweet Mustard

Trade Winds Gourmet Mustard is a sweet and hot mustard that can be used on a variety of foods, such as smoked meat, ham, pork, and outstanding on sausages. Check out our recipes for deviled eggs, meat loaves and potato salad. People that hate mustard love our product.

Ingredients:

Prepared Mustard (Distilled Vinegar, Water, No 1 Grade Mustard Seed, Salt, Contains less than 2% of Tumeric, Paprika, Spice, Natural Flavour, Garlic Powder), Vinegar, Bleached Wheat Flour Enriched (Niacin, Reduced Iron, Thalmine, Mononitrate, Riboflavin, Folic Acid), Malted Barley Flour (Contains Wheat, Soy), Sugar, Jalapenos. Packaged in 458g (16oz) bottles

HALIFAX SPICE RECIPIES

Square Rigger Tators
Wash and quarter potatoes. Leave peeling on. Rub with oil. Place in plastic bag and sprinkle with Tradewinds Spice Blend and shake until coated. Bake on cookie sheet @ 400 degrees for 45 minutes or until golden brown. Enjoy!
The Captain’s Bagel
Cut a bagel in two halves. Apply generous amount of cheddar cheese and sprinkle with Tradewinds Spice Blend. Heat in microwave until cheese melts. Delicious and quick!
High Seas Popcorn
Microwave your favorite popcorn. While still hot, sprinkle Tradewinds Spice Blend to taste. Adds a unique flavor!
Seadog Omelet
Blend two eggs together in a bowl. Sprinkle a small amount of Tradewinds Spice Blend while mixing. Cook in a skillet on low heat and add cheese, bacon bits, onions and green peppers or your own favorite ingredients.
Mate’s Chicken
Place chicken on broiler pan. Prepare chicken by piercing the skin with a sharp knife or fork. Sprinkle with Tradewinds Spice Blend. Add two strips of bacon over the chicken and cover with tin foil. Fold foil under edges of broiler pan. Cook @ 350 degrees until chicken is completely cooked. Remove foil and brown chicken. Juicy and delicious!
Nautical Pita Chips
Take a large pita and carefully separate with a knife into two flat pieces. Brush olive oil lightly on both sides of the separated pitas. Sprinkle with Tradewinds Spice Blend. Take pizza cutter and cut into triangles. Bake @ 375 for 7-10 minutes, watching they do not get too crispy. Dip in your favorite dip or salsa.
Galley Pizza
Same some of your Tradewinds Pasta Sauce to make delicious homemade pizza. Simply scoop a generous amount of Tradewinds Pasta Sauce on your pizza dough, cover with your favorite cheese and cook to perfection!
